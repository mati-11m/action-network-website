Best Practices.

Although Action Network is a recently established organization, it has done a good job of coordinating the 19 member of CSOs
and 8 other civil society organizations for the national dialogue "TOT" training conducted at Sidama National Regional State Hawassa.
Subsequently, the network coordinated and participated effectively with the seven committee members established at the district level in
order to be able to elect the right representatives for the nine community groups (constituencies) in order to set their key agendas for
the national dialogue and submit the detailed report to the commission.
Based on this, the commission evaluated the effectiveness of the
work, and the network was selected to coordinate others on behalf of the CSOs of the region. As a result, the network took the first round of training for the agenda setting at the next regional level.
