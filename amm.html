Action Network Structure

• General Assembly: The supreme organ of the network is the General Assembly.
• Board: Next to the General Assembly is the board of the network, which consists of five members and is charged with policy development and oversight functions.
• Auditor: There is also an auditor that follows the financial system of the network.
• Executive Director: Answerable to the board is the executive director, who serves as head of the secretariat charged with the management of the network.
• Technical Departments: Under the Executive Director, there are technical and administrative departments.
