•Establishment

• Action Network was established on 6th September 2022 as a Consortium of local CSOs with registration number 6397 in accordance with the Civil Society organizations proclamation No.1113/2019.

•Action Network Vision and Mission

Vision • •To see an empowered Civil Society sector that effectively contributes to the development and democratization process of the country.
Mission • •To build the capacities of its members, facilitate collaborative engagement among CSOs and with other stakeholders, voice issues of common concern on behalf of its members, lobby for creation of enabling environment and promote principles of peaceful coexistence, democratic values, human right and social cohesion among communities.
Action Network Values • Partnership and Collaboration • Accountability and transparency • Fairness • Impartiality • Value for resource • Mutual respect • Cultural sensitivity • Effectiveness and efficiency • Integrity
