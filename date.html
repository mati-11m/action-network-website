Objectives of the Network

• To create an enabling operational environment for CSOs operating in the area by fostering cooperation and coordination and cultivating a culture of dialogue among member organizations. • To create a conducive work environment for members through protection of their interests and rights, serving as a common voice platform. • To help member CSOs have transparent and accountable governance systems. • To develop the execution capacity and competencies of member organizations through training, coaching, and mentoring support, as well as the facilitation of experience sharing. • To enhance the role of member organizations by mobilizing resources from various sources. • To work towards strong information exchange between and among member organizations. • To work for members to have a relationship based on the principle of authentic partnership with the government and other development stakeholders. • To coordinate and direct members activities on issues like human rights, women's empowerment, protection of civilians, peace building, education, emergencies, and various development areas. The network has 19 CSO regular members and 4 honorary members. Members are the CSOs who have operational presence South Ethiopia. Action Network Structure • General Assembly: The supreme organ of the network is the General Assembly. • Board: Next to the General Assembly is the board of the network, which consists of five members and is charged with policy development and oversight functions. • Auditor: There is also an auditor that follows the financial system of the network. • Executive Director: Answerable to the board is the executive director, who serves as head of the secretariat charged with the management of the network. • Technical Departments: Under the Executive Director, there are technical and administrative departments. Policies and operational systems of the network The network bylaw, with 34 articles that were approved by the agency, was documented The network has in place a basic operational system and policies such as:- • Human Resource Management Manual • Financial Management Policy Manual • Fraud Policy • Compliant Handling Management Manual • Safeguarding Policy
